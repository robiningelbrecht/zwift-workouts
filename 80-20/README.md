# 80/20 Polarized Training

This schedule follows a **polarized intensity distribution**: ~80% of training time at low intensity (Zone 2) and ~20% at high intensity (VO₂max / threshold), with very little time in between.

The plan works well for riders training **4 or 5 days per week**, where the **Zone 2 ride is repeated multiple times**.

---

## Included Workouts

### 1. Over Unders (55min)
**Purpose:** Build sustainable power around FTP  
**Intensity:** High Zone 3 / Low Zone 4 (88–110% FTP)

2 sets of 6×(1min over / 1min under) with recovery between sets. This workout improves:
- Lactate clearance
- Muscular endurance
- Ability to hold steady power for longer efforts

Best done when relatively fresh.

---

### 2. 30/30 VO₂max (1h)
**Purpose:** Improve aerobic ceiling and repeatability  
**Intensity:** Very high (120% FTP during work intervals)

3 sets of 10×(30s on / 30s off) with 6min recovery between sets. This workout:
- Pushes VO₂max
- Improves ability to handle surges
- Trains fast power recruitment

This is the hardest workout of the week — treat it with respect.

---

### 3. Zone 2 (1h30)
**Purpose:** Aerobic base & endurance  
**Intensity:** Zone 2 (65% FTP)

This is the foundation of the program. Zone 2 work:
- Improves mitochondrial density
- Builds fatigue resistance
- Supports recovery between harder days

This workout is intentionally repeatable and should feel **easy enough to finish while still feeling good**.

---

## Weekly Structure

### Option A – 4 Days / Week
| Day   | Workout |
|-------|---------|
| Day 1 | Over Unders |
| Day 2 | Zone 2 |
| Day 3 | Rest |
| Day 4 | 30/30 VO₂max |
| Day 5 | Rest |
| Day 6 | Zone 2 |
| Day 7 | Rest |

---

### Option B – 5 Days / Week
| Day   | Workout |
|-------|---------|
| Day 1 | Over Unders |
| Day 2 | Zone 2 |
| Day 3 | Rest |
| Day 4 | Zone 2 |
| Day 5 | 30/30 VO₂max |
| Day 6 | Zone 2 |
| Day 7 | Rest |

The Zone 2 workout is intentionally repeated.
