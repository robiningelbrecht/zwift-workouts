# Pyramidal Training

This schedule follows a **pyramidal intensity distribution**: most time at low intensity, a meaningful block at moderate intensity (sweet spot), and a small amount at high intensity (VO₂max). The shape tapers from base to peak — more volume in the middle zones than a polarized plan.

The plan works well for riders training **4 or 5 days per week**, where the **Zone 2 ride is repeated multiple times**.

---

## Included Workouts

### 1. Sweet Spot 3×15 (1h30)
**Purpose:** Build sustained power just below FTP  
**Intensity:** Sweet spot (90% FTP)

3×15min at 90% FTP with 5min recovery between intervals. This workout:
- Builds muscular endurance with manageable fatigue
- Accumulates a large volume of quality work (45min at sweet spot)
- Improves time-to-exhaustion at threshold

Hard enough to drive adaptation, easy enough to recover from quickly.

---

### 2. 30/30 VO₂max (1h)
**Purpose:** Improve aerobic ceiling and repeatability  
**Intensity:** Very high (120% FTP during work intervals)

3 sets of 10×(30s on / 30s off) with 6min recovery between sets. This workout:
- Pushes VO₂max
- Improves ability to handle surges
- Trains fast power recruitment

This is the hardest workout of the week — treat it with respect.

---

### 3. Zone 2 (1h30)
**Purpose:** Aerobic base & endurance  
**Intensity:** Zone 2 (65% FTP)

This is the foundation of the program. Zone 2 work:
- Improves mitochondrial density
- Builds fatigue resistance
- Supports recovery between harder days

This workout is intentionally repeatable and should feel **easy enough to finish while still feeling good**.

---

## Weekly Structure

### Option A – 4 Days / Week
| Day   | Workout |
|-------|---------|
| Day 1 | Sweet Spot 3×15 |
| Day 2 | Zone 2 |
| Day 3 | Rest |
| Day 4 | 30/30 VO₂max |
| Day 5 | Rest |
| Day 6 | Zone 2 |
| Day 7 | Rest |

---

### Option B – 5 Days / Week
| Day   | Workout |
|-------|---------|
| Day 1 | Sweet Spot 3×15 |
| Day 2 | Zone 2 |
| Day 3 | Rest |
| Day 4 | Zone 2 |
| Day 5 | 30/30 VO₂max |
| Day 6 | Zone 2 |
| Day 7 | Rest |

The Zone 2 workout is intentionally repeated.
