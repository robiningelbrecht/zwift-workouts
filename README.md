This repository contains structured Zwift workouts organized into training schedules. Each schedule is designed for **4–5 days per week** with a focus on aerobic development.

## Schedules

| Schedule | Philosophy | Key Workouts |
|----------|-----------|--------------|
| [80/20 Polarized](80-20/) | ~80% low intensity, ~20% high intensity | Over Unders, 30/30 VO₂max, Zone 2 |
| [Pyramidal](pyramidal/) | Tapered distribution with sweet spot volume | Sweet Spot 3×15, 30/30 VO₂max, Zone 2 |

---

## How to Use These Workouts

1. Download the `.zwo` files from the schedule you want to follow
2. Place them in your Zwift workouts folder
    - **macOS:** `Documents/Zwift/Workouts/<your Zwift ID>/`
    - **Windows:** `Documents/Zwift/Workouts/<your Zwift ID>/`
3. Restart Zwift
4. Find the workouts under **Custom Workouts**

---

## Notes & Recommendations

- Make sure your **FTP is set correctly** in Zwift before starting.
- If fatigue accumulates, **drop one Zone 2 day**, not the hard workouts.
- These programs are best suited for:
    - Endurance-focused riders
    - Riders building fitness over multiple weeks
    - Cyclists who prefer structure without complexity

---

## Disclaimer

These workouts are provided as-is and assume you are healthy and accustomed to structured training.  
Listen to your body, fuel properly, and rest when needed.
